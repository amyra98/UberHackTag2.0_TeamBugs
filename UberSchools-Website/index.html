We Put Your Kids' Safety First

As parents ourselves, we only accept background-checked, fingerprinted drivers & sitters to whom we would entrust our own kids. They ride with Uber too!

Our drivers and helpers are local babysitters, moms, and teachers with 3-20 years of childcare experience, who have passed our comprehensive screening process.

  • Background checks
  • Fingerprinting
  • DMV record check
